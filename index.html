About Om Muruga Construction

Om Muruga Construction Company was established in 2004. The company was founded by Mr. A. Muthuraj and promoted by Eng. Muthukumar, B.E, a first-generation entrepreneur with over two decades of experience in construction and real estate development across Tamil Nadu.

With the mission of delivering globally comparable quality residential and commercial projects, the company follows the motto “Building Quality with Economy.”

“We Build Your Dream”

Om Muruga Construction is a professionally managed organization known for innovative design, superior construction standards, strong resource management, and customer satisfaction. Our commitment to quality and reliability ensures lasting value in every project we undertake.

Our comprehensive services include planning and designing, 3D elevation and plan approvals, residential and commercial construction, structural design, electrical and plumbing works, false ceiling, aluminum panels, MS and SS works, roofing sheets, water proofing and town planning consultancy.

About the Owner

Mr. Muthukumar, B.E is a qualified Civil Engineer and the promoter of OM Muruga Construction. He brings over 20+ years of overall experience in the construction industry, both in India and abroad, including 8+ years of international experience as a Civil Engineer.

His global exposure has strengthened his expertise in modern construction methodologies, international quality standards, safety practices, and efficient project execution. This experience enables the company to deliver projects that are durable, cost-effective, and built to global benchmarks.

Guided by strong professional ethics and a customer-centric approach, Mr. Muthukumar ensures that every project reflects Om Muruga Construction’s core promise — “We Build Your Dream.”
